The Baby Adventures App


Do you know the best little baby friendly restaurant in town? Are you looking for breastfeeding facilities in Dublin city centre? Or do you want to find out where you can bring your little baby when your out and about. The Baby Adventure App is here for all your questions and Answers. For the first time, there is a community based app to reveal all the best secrets of being out and about with your baby and young children.


As well as a community based feedback blog, we have an approved list of breastfeeding facilities around the country. Now you can view and assess where you want to go with your baby when its time for a feed. Now you have the freedom to plan your day out and about with your baby.
